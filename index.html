2. Customizing The Firmware

We take a deeper dive to ensure every piece fits together so your app runs seamlessly.

How We Do It

We start at the circuit board level & work our way up. You get access to customizable drivers, firmware, lockdown features & even the hardware modifications, such as camera sensors, RFID readers & more.

App First, Device Second

At Mason, we know how important it is for your application to run perfectly. That’s why our platform is designed with application uptime as our #1 priority.

3
Deploy Effortlessly

We can launch your network of dedicated devices with most carriers as well as WiFi-only. With Mason as your platform, you can easily dial up or down the hardware services that power your application. Services include firmware support & updates, device warranty, hardware replacement, network life-cycle management, and data connectivity.
